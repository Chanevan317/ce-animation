Step size and Noise effect

Step size effect quantizes a continuous signal by approximating its values at specific intervals, with a finer step size providing greater precision in representation.

Noise effect adds random variations to the signal, simulating disturbances or inaccuracies, which are then reduced through methods like filtering to approximate the original continuous signal.
